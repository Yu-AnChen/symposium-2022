We welcome everyone in the broadly defined fields of biomedical studies, life sciences, biology, and biotechnology to present their work. Each year, the symposium brings together people from a variety of different professional backgrounds and bridges expertise across biotech subfields. This would be an excellent venue for you to share your research beeyond your specific scope of study, to discuss and brainstorm with other young professionals, and to establish potential collaborations.

In addition to a poster presentation, you will also have the opportunity to give a 20-minute oral presentation (15-minute talk plus 5-minute QA) in the annual symposium. Please indicate your interest in giving a talk in your abstract submission. We will select 3 speakers from the pool of poster applications to give a talk. The selected presenters will be awarded a gift card ($30 in value) and their registration fee will be waived.

We will also select one poster for a most popular poster award based on the votes of the audience.

Please note: due to space limitations, a person can only submit one poster abstract and be the main presenting author on one poster. (You can be co-authors on multiple posters but can only be the main presenter of ONE poster.)
