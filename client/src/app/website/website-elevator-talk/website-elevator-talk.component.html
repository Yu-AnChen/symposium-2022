The 5-minute elevator talk competition includes an abstract competition and an oral competition.

Abstract competition

The abstracts should be submitted no later than September 30th. Each abstract will be judged by at least 3 referees with related expertise. The top 3 contestants will receive travel funds ($50 per person), and the selected 10 finalists will enter oral competition.

Oral competition

The elevator talk is on November 4th from 5:30 to 6:30 pm. Those selected will give a 5-minute presentation to highlight their research. The participants will be judged by a panel of 3 or more judges. Each judge scores speakers on a scale of 1 to 10 in each of the 3 criteria below. The judges' scores are combined and the top 3 are winners. The participants will be judged on the following criteria:

  • Comprehension: Does the presentation help the audience understand the research?
  • Engagement: Does the oration make the audience want to know more?
  • Communication: Is the information delivered clearly and simply?

1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners will be awarded $150, $100, and $50 accordingly.
